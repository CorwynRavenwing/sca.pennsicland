About Land Agents:

Each group camping at Pennsic must have a Land Agent.

A Land Agent needs:
• web access, to register on the Land Office website, and
• an e-mail account, to negotiate with the other Land Agents on their block before Pennsic

Land Agents should familiarize themselves with all the Land Rules and Procedures.

Land Agents should be in touch with everyone who will camp with their group. The group will be allocated space based on the number of pre-registered campers.

All Land Agents must be present sometime between Friday afternoon and Saturday morning by 9am to claim and rope off their land.
