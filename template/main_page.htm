About Land Agents:

Each group camping at Pennsic must have a Land Agent.

A Land Agent needs:
• web access, to register on the Land Office website, and
• an e-mail account, to negotiate with the other Land Agents on their block before Pennsic

Land Agents should familiarize themselves with all the Land Rules and Procedures.

Land Agents should be in touch with everyone who will camp with their group. The group will be allocated space based on the number of paid, pre-registered campers.

All Land Agents must check in at both the Troll and the Land Office. Land Agents or their registered Proxy must be available to negotiate and sign off on their block map sometime between at 9:00 am and at 10:00 am. After 10am Land One may negotiate with other land agents to finalize your group's location on the block.
