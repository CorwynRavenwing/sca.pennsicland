Group Name Selection

If your group has been registered with the Pennsic Land System before at a previous Pennsic War, even if you were not the Land Agent then, click on Existing. If this is the first year your group is registering with the Pennsic Land System, select New.

Group Status:

Your camping history and seniority are listed under your group record, so it is very important that if you are an EXISTING group, you click on Existing above, and not enter your group name here as if you were a new group.

If you are a NEW group please remember that your history from this time forward will be indexed by the group name you choose, and your group will be referenced in our system by that name.

Your group name may be up to 30 characters long, and have numbers, letters, and hyphens. Please spell out numbers (that is, "House 3Skulls" should be "House Three Skulls").
